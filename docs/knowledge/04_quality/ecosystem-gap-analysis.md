## Methodology

For each ecosystem AxiomID integrates with or claims compatibility for, we trace:
1. **Ecosystem Requirement** — what does the platform/standard require?
2. **AxiomID Claim** — what do we say we support?
3. **Implementation Reality** — what actually exists in code?
4. **Gap** — what's missing between claim and reality?
5. **Severity** — how critical is the gap?
6. **Recommendation** — what action to close the gap?
